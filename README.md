# EV-Market-Analysis-PowerBI
Power BI project analyzing India’s EV market for strategic decision-making.

# Electric Vehicle Market Analysis – AtliQ Motors

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Electric Vehicle (EV) market in India to support AtliQ Motors’ EV and hybrid vehicle expansion strategy. The dashboard provides insights into sales growth, EV penetration, regional opportunities, and future projections.

## Business Objectives
- Analyze EV sales and penetration trends in India
- Identify high-growth states for market entry
- Support manufacturing location and expansion decisions
- Provide strategic insights for top management

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Interactive Dashboards)
- SQL (Data Preparation)
- Excel (Data Cleaning)

## Files Included
- Power BI (EV_project challenge.pbix) file
- Dashboard screenshots
- SQL queries

## Key Insights
- EV penetration is increasing steadily across major states
- Certain states show strong adoption due to policy support and infrastructure
- Growth trends indicate strong future demand for EVs

## Market & Competition Analysis
Ola Electric leads in EV sales among makers, securing the 1st position. Maharashtra ranks as the top state in EV adoption with the highest sales contribution. Across financial years FY22, FY23, and FY24, FY24 recorded the highest EV sales, showing strong year-on-year growth and increasing market demand.
![Dashboard Screenshot](https://github.com/githama-barveen/EV-Market-Analysis-PowerBI/blob/main/Screenshots/Market%20%26%20Competition%20Analysis.png)

## Regional Opportunity Analysis
Insight : Maharashtra and Kerala are the long-term penetration leader Maharashtra ranks #1 in current penetration and also dominates the FY 2030 projection, indicating both scale and sustained momentum. 
Kerala consistently stays in the top tier, showing strong adoption maturity rather than short-term spikes. 
Business implication: These states are low-risk, high-return markets for expansion, partnerships, and policy-led EV investments.
![Dashboard Screenshot](https://github.com/githama-barveen/EV-Market-Analysis-PowerBI/blob/main/Screenshots/Regional%20Opportunity%20Analysis.png)

## Strategic Analysis
4-Wheeler EV penetration recorded a strong 83% YoY growth from FY23 to FY24, indicating rapid acceleration in adoption.  This surge suggests increased impact of government incentives and improved cost viability, driving customer confidence in EV ownership.
States with higher EV penetration also record higher sales, indicating that infrastructure readiness supports scalable adoption. Improved charging availability helps convert early adoption into sustained growth.
